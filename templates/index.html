How to Use

  1. Enter News: On the homepage, you’ll find a text box where you can paste or type the news article or statement you wish to verify.
  2. Submit Your News: Click the Verify button to send your input for processing.
  3. Processing: The system will fetch related news from multiple sources using the Google News API and Serper tool, then use AI agents to analyze and cross-reference the information.
  4. View Verification Results: Once processing is complete, you’ll see a detailed report. This includes a final verdict, reasoning behind the analysis, and clickable source links for further verification.
  5. Review Additional Context: The results also provide background information—such as historical context, key statistics, and related events—to help you understand the authenticity of the news.
  6. Try Another Verification: If you want to verify a different news piece, simply return to the homepage and repeat the process.

Note: Ensure that you enter a valid news article or statement before clicking verify. If you encounter any errors, double-check your input and try again.
